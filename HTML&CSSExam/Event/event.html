Chief Guests

K.chiranjeevi

Film Actor

Konidela Chiranjeevi is an Indian actor, film producer and former politician who works predominantly in Telugu cinema. Born Konidela Sivasankara Varaprasad; 22 August 1955. On May 9, Chiranjeevi graced the grand stage of Rashtrapati Bhavan in Delhi, donning his signature black suit as he received the Padma Vibhushan from the President of India.

M.M.keeravani

Singer

Koduri Marakathamani Keeravaani (born 4 July 1961), professionally known as M. M. Keeravani, is an Indian music composer, record producer, singer and lyricist, who predominantly works in Telugu Cinema. Keeravani's 'Naatu Naatu' became the first Indian film song to win an Oscar.
