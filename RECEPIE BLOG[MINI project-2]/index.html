Chicken Fajitas Recipe

★ ★ ★ ★ (4 out of 5)

Ingredients

  • 1 box of pasta (penne, macaroni, etc.)
  • 2 cups shredded cheese (mozzarella, cheddar, or a blend)
  • 2 cups tomato sauce (marinara, pasta sauce, or your favorite)
  • (Optional) 1 cup chopped vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, spinach)
  • (Optional) ½ cup milk (for a creamier sauce)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Spices: Dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and cook vegetables (if using) until softened.
  4. Add tomato sauce, spices, and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. (Optional) Add milk for a creamier sauce.
  6. Combine cooked pasta, cheese, and sauce. Pour into a greased baking dish.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until bubbly and golden brown.

Cooking Tips

  • For a crispy topping, add extra cheese and bake for 5 minutes longer.
  • Substitute ground meat or cooked chicken for vegetables (optional).
  • Experiment with different cheeses and vegetables for variations.

chicken with broccoli

★ ★ ★ ★ ★(5 out of 5)

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts (or thighs), thinly sliced
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 1/2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying (enough to fill a skillet)

Instructions

  1. Prepare three shallow bowls: one for flour, one for beaten eggs, and one for panko breadcrumbs. Season the flour with salt and pepper.
  2. Dredge each chicken slice in the flour, then dip it in the beaten eggs, and finally coat it evenly with panko breadcrumbs.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat (around 350°F/175°C). Don't overcrowd the pan, fry the chicken in batches for even cooking and golden brown color.
  4. Fry for 3-4 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through. Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.

Cooking Tips

  • For extra crispy chicken, double coat the chicken in the egg and panko breadcrumbs.
  • Use a thermometer to monitor the oil temperature. Avoid overheating the oil, which can burn the chicken.
  • Serve chicken fry hot with your favorite dipping sauces like ketchup, mustard, or hot sauce.
  • Leftover chicken fry can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for a few minutes until crispy.
