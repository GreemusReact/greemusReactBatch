Destinations

Haryana

The North-Indian state of Haryana is a blend of all the riches and rags you can see in one place.

Chandigarh

Rock Garden, Sukhna Lake, Zakir Hussain Rose Garden, Elante Mall, Chhatbir Zoo, Government Museum and Art Gallery, Chandigarh, Open Hand Monument, Chandigarh Capitol Complex, etc.

Delhi

Delhi is unique and ultimate in every aspect! From historical landmarks to the charm of Old Delhi and the yummiest street food, Delhi is the place to be for all the tourists to visit when on a tour around India.

Himachal Pradesh

The beautiful terrain of mountains in Himachal Pradesh is a sight for sore eyes? This mountain landscape at the foothills of the Himalayas is a geographical marvel with valleys, plains and snow-capped mountains.

Jammu And Kashmir

Heaven on Earth or Jammu And Kashmir, one or the same thing. However, due to political upheavals in the place, people very often avoid it but this is one place that you should visit at least once in your lifetime.

Punjab

The name of the place itself speaks volumes of energy, non-veg spicy food, prosperity, and greenery. The state of Punjab in the lap of the northern plains of India is worth a visit for all the food lovers.

Rajasthan

This royal and colourful state is on top of the list for any non-Indian traveller visiting India. The vibes of the place are nothing like you'll experience in any other place. One of the most visited states of India, the place is exotic.

Uttarakhand

Towering Himalayan Peaks, scenic hill stations, water bodies, and unspoiled natural terrains make Uttarakhand one of the most visited and best tourist places in India. Ancient holy places, forests and valleys, national parks

Uttar Pradesh

Not your ideal tourist place, except as the infamous location of Taj Mahal, this place has much more to offer. This spiritual and historical destination has a lot to offer, historical spots, delish sweets, scenic riversides and holy sites.

Dadar and Nagar Haveli

Another union territory in western India wedged between Maharashtra and Gujarat is a heaven for nature lovers. A sylvan paradise that derives its' name from the Portuguese word silva, which means the wood is a place for spectacular beauty.

Daman And Diu

Another worthy tourist place in India, Daman and Diu in Gujarat i.e. West India consists of two separate areas divided by the Arabian Sea. The beautiful place surrounded by the Arabian Sea and the Daman Ganga River is a part of the country that is both culturally and scenically beautiful.

Goa

Without any doubt, one of the most popular tourist places in India, Goa is the state to beat! Beaches, bars, shacks, dancing, drinks, karaoke, adventure sports, shopping, history, food and the list goes on.
